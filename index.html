Hello! Welcome to my about me page!

I was originally born in Maryland. My family and I are all proud Deaf people! Throughout my childhood, I grew up near Boulder, Colorado. At age 12, my parents decided to move to Israel. I have spent there almost half of my life (11 years!) and recently moved back on my own to the states to get myself a bachelors degree!


My hobbies:

  • I ride motorcycles! My most favorite type is japanese sport bikes :)
  • I am a licensed open water scuba diver. I love diving and using sign language under water!!
  • I love doing mountain treks too! I've done two long treks in my life, 7 days trek in Huayhuash Circuit and 5 days in Salkantay Circuit.
